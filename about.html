Our Story

Built for the Nigerian Market

Supamart was founded with a simple belief — that buying and selling online should be safe, simple, and accessible to everyone in Nigeria and across Africa.

Under Supper Needs International Ltd, we built a platform where verified sellers can showcase their products to thousands of buyers, while buyers enjoy a curated, trustworthy shopping experience managed by our dedicated admin team.

Every transaction, every inquiry, every order is handled with care — because your experience matters to us.
